Which German Level Do You Actually Need?

Most students come to us unsure of which exam to take. Here's the quick guide.

A1

Just starting out

You need this if: applying for an Au Pair visa, a German spouse / family reunification visa, or just starting your German journey.

~80–100 hours of study · 2–3 months with us

A2

Everyday conversation

You need this if: applying for Ausbildung programmes that require A2, or building a foundation before pushing to B1.

~150–200 hours total · 3 months with us

B1

The most-asked-for level

You need this if: applying for most Ausbildung programmes, German citizenship, or a job seeker visa. The minimum for most working visas.

~350–400 hours total · 4–6 months with us

B2

Study or work in Germany

You need this if: applying for a Bachelor's or Master's at a German university, or a skilled-worker visa in healthcare, IT, or engineering.

~600 hours total · 5–6 months with us
