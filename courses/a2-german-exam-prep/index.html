Your A2 Journey

A focused 3-month path to A2 — building from A1 foundations to exam readiness.

Month 1 — Grammar Consolidation

Past tense (Perfekt and Präteritum for key verbs), modal verbs, two-way prepositions, separable verbs, and the A2 vocabulary sets from the official word lists.

Month 2 — Reading, Listening & Writing

A2-format reading strategies (short notices, ads, emails), listening to dialogues and short talks, and writing structured short texts — messages, notes, and simple letters.

Month 3 — Speaking Practice & Mock Exams

Structured Sprechen partner sessions, two full mock exams under exam conditions, detailed feedback on every section, and a final exam strategy session.
