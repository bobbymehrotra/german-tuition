Your A1 Journey

A structured 2–3 month path from zero to passing your A1 exam.

Weeks 1–4 — Foundations

The German alphabet and pronunciation, numbers, greetings, introductions, and basic personal information. Core grammar: present tense verbs, articles, and simple sentence structure.

Weeks 5–8 — Exam Skills

A1-format reading texts and listening exercises. Short writing tasks (forms, messages, simple emails). Sprechen practice with A1 question types: describing pictures, role-plays.

Weeks 9–12 — Mock Exams & Final Prep

Two full timed A1 mock exams under real exam conditions. Detailed section-by-section feedback, targeted revision of weak areas, and exam-day strategy session.
