# BoloGerman

> Online Goethe and telc German exam coaching for A1, A2, B1, and B2. Run by Bobby Mehrotra — MBA from a German university, 10+ years of teaching experience. Based in Delhi, classes conducted fully online across India.

## About

BoloGerman is a specialist online coaching service focused exclusively on Goethe and telc German language exam preparation. The founder and lead tutor, Bobby Mehrotra, lived and worked in Germany for nearly a decade, holds an MBA from a German university, and has been teaching German for over 10 years. Classes are conducted in small online batches for personal attention, with mock tests and structured exam strategies.

## Services

### Goethe / telc A1 German Exam Preparation
For students applying for Au Pair visas, German spouse / family reunification visas, or just beginning their German journey. Approximately 2–3 months of coaching covering all four exam sections: Lesen (reading), Hören (listening), Schreiben (writing), and Sprechen (speaking).

### Goethe / telc A2 German Exam Preparation
For students applying for Ausbildung programmes requiring A2, or building a foundation before B1. Approx. 3 months.

### Goethe / telc B1 German Exam Preparation
The most in-demand level. Required for most Ausbildung programmes, German citizenship, and job seeker visas. Approx. 4–6 months.

### Goethe / telc B2 German Exam Preparation
For students applying for a Bachelor's or Master's degree at a German university, or skilled-worker visas in healthcare, IT, or engineering. Approx. 5–6 months.

### School German Tuition
Curriculum-based German support for school students in classes 6–10, covering grammar and school exam preparation.
