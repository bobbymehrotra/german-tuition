Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to learn German?

The time varies depending on your starting level and goals. Beginners typically see conversational progress in 3-6 months, while reaching fluency can take 1-2 years with consistent practice.

What materials are included?

All students receive access to textbooks, workbooks, audio materials, and online resources. We also provide practice exercises and cultural content to enhance your learning experience.

Can I get a free trial lesson?

Absolutely! We offer a free 30-minute consultation to assess your current level, discuss your goals, and answer any questions you might have about our teaching approach.

Do you prepare students for exams?

Yes, we offer specialized preparation for various German language exams including TestDaF, DSH, Goethe-Zertifikat, and others. Our success rate is excellent!
